# Deep Sea Diver

## Objective
Recover the Abyssal Core from the trench and return to the surface alive. Your oxygen depletes over time, and a security drone patrols the Pressure Gate.

## How to Play
You navigate the world by typing short commands. For example, 'd' for down, 'e' for east. 
You can type the long-form version of commands, but note that the command parser expects the short-form abbreviations as well. 
If you are ever stuck, press Enter with no input to receive a hint.

## Winning Path (Walkthrough)
Here is the step-by-step sequence of commands to recover the core and win the game:

1. `d` - Move down from the Surface Platform to the Airlock.
2. `we` - Move west to the Equipment Locker.
3. `op lk` - Open the locker.
4. `ta su` - Take the reinforced diving suit.
5. `wr su` - Wear the reinforced diving suit (required to survive the trench).
6. `e` - Move east back to the Airlock.
7. `d` - Move down to the Pressure Gate.
8. `so ox` - Solve the oxygen puzzle to unlock the gate (Note: Do not type "solve oxygen" as there is a bug in the parser mapping!).
9. `e` - Move east into the Abyssal Trench.
10. `ta co` - Take the Abyssal Core.
11. `we` - Move west back to the Pressure Gate.
12. `u` - Move up to the Airlock.
13. `u` - Move up to the Surface Platform to escape and win the game!
